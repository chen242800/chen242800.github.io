Here is a project that I involved for one year. Our team has 7 members and 2 clients. Our aim is to develop an online museum -- GuideXP.

GuideXP has four attributes that break through the barriers of space and time, enabling easy access to the Internet at any time and any place over a vast area, integrates the resources of the physical museum, and uses multimedia to create vivid, image and vivid display effect, make knowledge learning more convenient and systematic, the interactive and immersive design would make every visitor feel as if they are in the real world.
