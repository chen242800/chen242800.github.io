Page View

PV is the abbreviation of page view, that is, page views. It is usually the main index to measure a network news channel or website or even a network news. The number of page views is one of the most commonly used indicators to evaluate website traffic, which is called PV for short. Monitoring the change trend of website PV and analyzing the reasons for its change are the work that many webmasters have to do on a regular basis. Page in page views generally refers to ordinary HTML web pages, and also includes dynamic HTML content such as PHP and JSP. An HTML content request from the browser will be regarded as a PV and gradually accumulated into the total PV.
